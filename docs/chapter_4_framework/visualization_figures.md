- Figure 4.x -- Action Boundary Plot. Shows rho relative to accept, lower_confidence and audit thresholds.
- Figure 4.x -- Operator Trace Heatmap Compact. Compares experiments by gamma, delta, rho and dominant components.

- Figure 4.x -- Proof Consistency Matrix. Shows consistency of route, trace, proof, dashboard data, verifier and manifest.
